    THETA V Plugin Application Development Overview

    The THETA V uses Android as the operating system. Because of this, the THETA V application development process is the same as the development process for an Android phone. Connect the THETA V to a development workstation, either Mac, Windows, or Linux with a micro USB cable and connect to the camera with adb. You will need a THETA V that is in developer mode. To put your THETA V into developer mode during the closed-beta period, please contact jcasman@oppkey.com. He will need to get physical access to your camera. More information on a process to put the THETA V into developer mode remotely should be available when the THETA V partner program is launched in 2018 (specific date of launch has not been announced).
